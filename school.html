My Education

The Name of my school is Penckers . It is located in Adebisi Omotola St, Isolo,Lagos. Penckers Schools is a co-educational Muslim school running nursery and primary school academic programmes using the Nigerian and British curriculum. It is located in Isolo, Lagos and equipped with all the necessary facilities for teaching and learning in the 21st century. Penckers is a private institution which provides world class education currently running a pre primary and primary programs ,Penckers mean pen creates kings leaders and scholars. Penckers is one of the schools in Nigeria currently approved and recognized by qualification and curriculum development authority (Q.C.D.A.) United Kingdom. PENCKERS SCHOOL provides high quality education propelled by islamic values under the most conducive atmosphere possible. Our curriculum is a studied hybrid of the British and Nigerian educational programmes with a flavour of the pedagogical strategies advocated by Montessori Jolly phonics and the likes, all of which are utilised at the appropriate levels of learning.
